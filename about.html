About Loons & Leaves

Loons & Leaves is an indie wellness and fitness-focused software brand based in Canada. We design simple, focused tools to help people move better, train smarter, and stay motivated — together.

Meet Breut

Breut is the lead developer and creative force behind Loons & Leaves. A lifelong learner with a deep love for strength training, Breut brings together fitness knowledge and technical skill to build digital tools that empower others on their health journey.

From developing smart weightlifting utilities to collaborative team fitness apps, Breut’s passion is helping others find joy in movement, discipline in training, and progress they can feel.

Whether you're tracking your barbell setup with Rack It or joining your team on a long-distance challenge in Miles Together, our mission is to make progress feel satisfying, beautiful, and fun.
